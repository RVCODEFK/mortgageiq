Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the mortgage calculator?

Our calculator uses the standard mortgage amortization formula used by banks and financial institutions worldwide, providing bank-grade accuracy for all calculations.

Is my financial data stored or shared?

No. All calculations happen in your browser using JavaScript. We don't collect, store, or share your financial information. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use this calculator for professional purposes?

Absolutely! MortgageIQ is designed for homebuyers, real estate professionals, financial advisors, and anyone needing accurate mortgage calculations. The tool is free and has no usage limits.

What currencies do you support?

We support 12+ international currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, CHF, MXN, BRL, COP, ARS, and CLP.

Do you offer an API or embeddable widget?

We don't currently offer an API or widget, but this is on our roadmap. Contact us if you're interested in partnership opportunities.
